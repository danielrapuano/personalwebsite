Various University Projects

During my attendance as a computer science major, I worked in multiple groups to create various applications from start to finish.
The first of these was for a UX/UI course that required us to reimagine a pre-existing website for a restaurant.
While simple, it implements several key design concepts pertaining to optimized human-computer interaction including
proper signaling through bordering and spacing, standard iconography for signaling, and recognizable page structure. I
additionally worked on an AI generative application that served to provide small businesses the ability to create
customizalbe advertisements utilizing the OpenAI API, and then publish these ads to the social media of their choosing.
I primarily worked on the frontend for this project alongside two others focusing on backend and database development.
I had to implement components such as form overlays, folder structure display, and overall styling/ design choices.
Both of these projects were limited to a semester, so time was limited. In the future I intend to modify these projects
to better reflect a more robust and clean user interface.
